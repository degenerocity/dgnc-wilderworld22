# Wilder World Cribs

South Beach is the first district being developed in Wilder World and the central hub of Wiami. 
Known for its beautiful beaches where Wilders come from far and wide to enjoy the newest cultural hot spot of the Metaverse.

With the launch of South Beach also comes the launch of **The Qube**. The Qube is our genesis Real Estate release in Wilder World. Consisting of 108 unique procedurally generated luxury NFT condos spread across 39 floors reaching up into the Wiami heavens.
